With over 290,000 club members, FC Bayern München eV is the sports club boasting the largest number of members in the world. Through their membership, our supporters not only document their identification with the club, but are also part of the large FC Bayern family.

Your heart also beats for FC Bayern Munich and you would like to be part of the red and white club family? Then apply for  membership  now - we look forward to welcoming you!

You can find all the important information about membership here on this page.

Oh, and by the way:

100% of the income generated by memberships benefits FC Bayern München eV, which primarily supports our youth work and our grassroots sports departments.
